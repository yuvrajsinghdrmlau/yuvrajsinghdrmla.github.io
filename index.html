Research

Springer Publication

Peer Reviewed · Springer
Infrared Small Target Detection Using Deep Learning: A Multi-Scale Attention Approach for Low-SNR Environments
Springer — Journal / Conference Proceedings · 2026
This paper addresses the challenge of detecting small, dim infrared targets embedded in complex clutter backgrounds — a critical problem in defence surveillance and early-warning systems. We evaluate multi-scale attention-based architectures (MPANet) against optimised YOLO variants, demonstrating superior detection rates under low signal-to-noise ratio conditions. The work bridges classical signal processing techniques with modern deep learning on real sensor data, contributing methods applicable to both defence and civilian remote sensing domains.
